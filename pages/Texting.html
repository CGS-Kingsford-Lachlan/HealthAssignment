The dangers of distraction

Driving is a complex task. If something takes your mind or eyes off the road, not only will it compromises your safety, but that of everyone else on the road. Even if you are just looking at a message for a slpit second at the speed of 72kmph you would have traveled 20 meters, thats enough time to not see something cross your path.

Being distracted increases your chances of having a crash. It slows down your reaction times and puts you in danger of failing to see hazards such as traffic lights, stop signs or other road users, including pedestrians and cyclists. It is better for you to use a bluetooth handset so you can keep your hands on the wheel, but your mind will become more distracted.
